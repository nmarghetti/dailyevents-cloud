Keep up with quotidian events!

Friends and co-workers want to meet with you. Participate in local events by joining existing groups, or create new groups and invite people in. Confirm or cancel your attendance, and discuss about today's session with other members.

Day-based

Within a time window of 24h, set the place and time with your peers.

Check-in

Notify people whether you are coming today or not anymore.

Comments

Engage discussions, or warn members if you are arriving late.

Requires .NET or Mono
